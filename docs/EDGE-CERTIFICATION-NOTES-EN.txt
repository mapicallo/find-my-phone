Find my phone — certification notes (English, paste into Partner Center)

GENERAL
Find my phone is a Manifest V3 extension. It does not require login to our service and has no backend. A single click opens the Microsoft Edge toolbar action, which opens or focuses a floating panel. From the panel the user chooses Google Find My Device (Android) or Apple iCloud Find (iPhone). The extension opens the official provider URLs in a popup window. The user signs in on Google or Apple as they normally would. No test accounts are provided by the publisher.

HOW TO TEST
1) Install the package and pin the extension if needed.
2) Click the toolbar icon. A floating, movable panel appears with Android / iPhone buttons and an optional language selector (English / Spanish).
3) Tap Android or iPhone. A popup window opens with the official Google or Apple locator URL. Sign in with your own Google or Apple account if prompted to verify ring/locate flows.
4) Optional: enable "Remember my platform" and use the one-click shortcut on a later visit.

PERMISSIONS
• storage — Persists UI language and optional "remember platform" choice only (chrome.storage.local).
• windows — Opens or reuses the floating panel window (chrome.windows).

DATA / PRIVACY
No analytics and no accounts with the publisher. The extension does not read page content or access credentials; authentication happens only on Google or Apple sites.
