Privacy Policy

Find my phone · Last updated: 2025

1. Summary

The Find my phone extension does not collect, store, or send personal data to external servers. Everything stays in your browser.

2. Data used by the extension

The extension uses Chrome's storage permission to save only:

  • Preferred language (Spanish or English)
  • Preferred platform (Android or iPhone)

This data is stored only on your device, in the browser's local storage. It is not sent to any server.

3. How it works

When you click a button, the extension opens a popup window with:

  • Android: Google Find My Device
  • iPhone: iCloud Find Devices

The extension does not access your Google or Apple accounts. You manage authentication directly on those pages.

4. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can open an issue in the project repository or contact the developer.
